How to play

  1. Click and drag on shooter to aim and set power, then release to fire.
  2. Balls bounce off walls and break blocks until they lose momentum.
  3. Clear all blocks to advance. Some blocks need multiple hits.

Tips

  • Aim at angles to produce multi-bounce hits.
  • Use power for long shots; low power gives controlled bounces.
  • Higher levels have tougher bricks — watch HP bars.
